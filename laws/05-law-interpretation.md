# Law Interpretation

## Minimal and implementation-neutral
Open RTA laws define the minimum conditions for accountable autonomy. They intentionally avoid prescribing architecture, language, deployment model, or orchestration style.

## Laws are not product features
The laws are compliance constraints, not feature marketing claims. A runtime is aligned when evidence demonstrates the constraints are met.

## Laws are testable constraints
Each law should be evaluated through inspectable artifacts, not by assertion alone.

## No silent expansion from one runtime
No single runtime's preferences or implementation details should be treated as universal law. Law changes must occur through documented governance.
