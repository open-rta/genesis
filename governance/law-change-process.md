# Law Change Process

## What is a law-level change
A law-level change alters legal meaning, obligations, or compliance interpretation of one or more laws.

## Additional requirements
Law-level proposals must include:
- explicit rationale for the change
- alternatives considered
- compatibility and migration impact
- examples of newly compliant or noncompliant behavior

## Review standard
Law-level changes require broader review than non-law clarifications and should include participants from multiple runtime contexts.

## Publication
Accepted law changes are versioned, recorded in changelog, and linked to a decision record.
