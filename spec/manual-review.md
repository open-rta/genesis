# Manual Review Scope

Automatic validation is necessary but insufficient for certification-grade assurance.

## What remains manual

Manual/foundation review evaluates:
- whether oversight is meaningful (not merely present)
- whether control mechanisms are genuinely useful and timely
- whether replay artifacts support practical reconstruction
- honesty and completeness of declared scope/limitations
- quality and consistency of operational practice over time
- any higher-assurance criteria required for certification decisions

## Relationship to validator output

A passing validator report indicates structural and prerequisite readiness for review.

It does **not** by itself establish L4 certification or formal endorsement.
