# Authority

Authority identifies who or what is permitted to authorize autonomous behavior.

## Who or what authorizes action
Authority may be a person, team, service principal, policy system, or other governance mechanism.

## Authority vs execution
Authority grants permission and constraints. Execution performs actions. These roles may be linked but should remain conceptually distinct.

## Why authority lineage matters
Lineage explains originator, approver, and executor relationships. This supports accountability and review.
